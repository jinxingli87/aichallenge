Scholarship Overview

CESASC is one of the largest and the most established Chinese-American professional organizations in Southern California. Founded in 1962, CESASC has been dedicated to promoting interests, aspirations, and professional excellence of Chinese-American engineers and scientists. CESASC provides career and educational advancement opportunities, technical exchange, fellowship and community service.

In the past ten years, CESASC has given away more than $400,000 in scholarships awarded to more than four hundred excellent students. This year, CESASC will continue the tradition and offer 10 general scholarships and 3 Lisa Lu Foundation (盧燕基金會) scholarships for awardees majoring in literature and arts.

Please refer to the FAQ section below for all necessary details. If you have any further questions, please email cesasc@gmail.com.

Applicant Notes

All scholarship recipients will be invited to the CESASC Annual Convention and to network with professionals in the fields. Graduate student recipients may also be invited to present their research work at the CESASC Annual Convention.

All awardees need to present their research at the Interdisciplinary Talk (2:30 – 5:00 pm) and attend the CESASC 2026 Convention Gala (starting 6:00 pm) to receive awards. Awardees are encouraged to join CESASC as members and to attend future CESASC events.

Regardless of the results, CESASC encourages all applicants to stay involved and participate in future CESASC activities and be part of the CESASC family. Please help notify your fellow students about this opportunity. Best of luck in your educational endeavor.

南加州中華科工學會大學生/研究生獎學金申請辦法

南加州中華科工學會自1962年成立以來，給數百位在美國各大學學習的大學生和研究生頒發過獎學金，目前一些早年的得獎人已經在各自的行業中頗有建樹。 該會將繼續給合格的申請人頒發獎學金，現在申請資料已經公布在本學會網上，申請的截止日期是2026年3月13日。

凡是正在美國大學(二年級以上)本科或研究所學習的學生都可以填表申請該獎學金。今年，科工會提供10項科工會獎學金，以及3項盧燕基金會獎學金。 有關獎學金申請的各種細節，請進一步參考FAQ文件。若有進一步的問題，請聯系：cesasc@gmail.com。

南加州中華科工學會獎學金委員會將根據申請人的學習成績、包括學術或非學術性獎勵、研究成果、經濟需要、學習目標和課外活動等條件來決定得獎人， 並將于2026年3月23日通知申請人得獎與否。每份獎學金1200美元。往年已得奖者不予考虑。

獲獎者須出席科工會2026年會。我們鼓勵獲獎者加入科工會成為會員，擔當科工會STEM項目志願者（比如，指導初/高中學生STEM論文比賽參與者）， 並幫助組織未來的科工會活動和年會。

無論得獎與否，期待所有申請人積極參加科工會活動，成為科工會的成員。科工學會獎學金是該學會社區服務的一個重要組成部分，希望學生及其父母借助科工學會的平台， 聯係南加州地區從事科學和工程領域的專業人士，發展各自的學業和事業，同時回饋社區。

2026 CESASC Scholarship Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  1. What are the history and purpose of the CESASC Scholarship? Chinese-American Engineers and Scientists Association of Southern California (CESASC), founded in 1962, is a leading Chinese-American professional organization promoting professional excellence in Southern California. CESASC provides annual scholarships on merit and financial need. Funding comes from corporate and individual donors and is managed by the CESASC Scholarship Foundation, a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization.
  2. Who can apply for the CESASC Scholarship? Sophomores and above or graduate students in a university or college are eligible. Students from non-Chinese descent and applicants from non-science/engineering majors are also welcome. Previous CESASC Scholarship awardees are not eligible.
  3. When is the deadline to apply for the CESASC Scholarship? Applications must be received by 23:59 Pacific Time on March 13, 2026.
  4. How do I apply? Submit the application online at https://cesasc.org/scholarship2026. Supporting materials (PDF preferred) include a resume of personal experience and publications (if any), academic transcripts (encouraged), and one letter of recommendation from a professor.
  5. How can I find out whether I am awarded with a scholarship/award? The scholarship awardees will be notified by email before March 23, 2026. Students who do not win the scholarship will also receive notification emails.
  6. Are there any other requirements for scholarship awardees? Awardees present their research work at an interdisciplinary talk and must attend the convention gala to receive awards.
  7. How should I communicate with the CESASC Scholarship committee? Please send an email to: cesasc@gmail.com.
